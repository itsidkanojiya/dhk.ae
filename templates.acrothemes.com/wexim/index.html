About DHK IT Solutions

DHK IT Solutions - SPCFZ, UAE

DHK IT Solutions is a UAE-registered IT and digital services company, officially launched in 2025 under Sharjah Publishing City Free Zone (SPCFZ). Though new to the UAE market, our roots go back over 5 years in India, where we built a strong foundation delivering high-quality web, cloud, and software solutions to a global client base.

We specialize in website design, app and web hosting, cloud computing, business software, database management, and IT consulting, serving startups, SMEs, educational institutions, and real estate agencies.

With a dual presence in the UAE and India, we combine cost-effective offshore development with local customer service and compliance.
